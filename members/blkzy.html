Hi, my name is Matheus and i'm 22 years old.


I've been in the hacking field since 2018, but in 2021 I had to take
a break, and I'm back now in 2024 focused on the blue team and intelligence.


My intention here is to share what little knowledge I have,
and also to help myself with writing texts.


My projects:

- wpreconx (archived) 

   This is a tool for collecting information and scanning WordPress sites.


   I started developing it to be an alternative to wpscan, but totally free.
   In the middle of the development process, I had to make it private
   due to its military use for detecting internal vulnerabilities.



- apkronx (archived)

   This was an epiphany of an idea related to the exploration of
   mobile applications, both Android (APK) and iOS (IPA).


   I simply got bored and decided to shelve the project, but in
   the future I intend to resurrect it and continue its development.


- spectrum (developing)

   This is my latest project, designed to collect information from
   the social networks of specific tangoes. The idea is to define
   a tango and set Spectrum to follow its life on social networks
   in real time.


   I thought of it to exercise my code writing skills in Python,
   Golang and also to improve my database skills.
